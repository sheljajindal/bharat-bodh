The Conclave

7, 8 , 9th February 2025

Guwahati, Assam

Bharat Bodh

A grand conclave that aims to celebrate and explore the diverse aspects of India's cultural heritage. The event will feature intellectual discussions, cultural performances, and exhibitions showcasing the richness of Indian traditions. The goal is to deepen the understanding of India's cultural legacy, philosophy, history, and literary traditions, while addressing modern challenges and opportunities related to the preservation and promotion of this heritage.

Objectives

Cultural Exploration: Focusing on India's cultural heritage through philosophical discussions, historical analysis, and literary exploration to foster a comprehensive understanding.
Interdisciplinary Dialogue: Bringing together scholars, researchers, and cultural practitioners for dialogue on India's cultural heritage and its global significance.
Global Networking: Creating a platform to foster meaningful connections among academic, cultural, and artistic professionals.
Heritage Conservation: Exploring strategies for preserving and promoting Indiaâ€™s heritage, especially in the digital age, addressing the challenges of globalization.
Cultural Performance: Showcasing classical and folk performances that reflect India's living cultural heritage.
Exhibitions and Workshops: Featuring exhibitions of traditional crafts, textiles, and other art forms, along with interactive workshops on traditional practices.
